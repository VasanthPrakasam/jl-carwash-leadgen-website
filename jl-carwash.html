Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a car wash take?
A standard car wash takes about 30–45 minutes. Premium packages with interior cleaning and polish may take 60–90 minutes. Bike washes are usually done in 20–30 minutes.
Do you use safe, eco-friendly products?
Yes! We use high-quality, eco-safe cleaning products that are gentle on your vehicle's paint and safe for the environment. Our products are biodegradable and non-toxic.
Is prior booking required?
No booking is required — walk-ins are always welcome! However, to guarantee your preferred time slot, especially during weekends, we recommend calling ahead or using the booking form above.
What vehicles do you service?
We service all types of vehicles including cars (hatchbacks, sedans, SUVs, vans), bikes, and scooters. For commercial vehicles, please call us directly for a custom quote.
Do you offer any membership or loyalty plans?
We offer special deals for regular customers. Call us or WhatsApp to ask about our monthly wash packages — especially popular with Ola/Uber drivers who visit weekly.
Where are you located exactly?
We're located at Pudhu Rd, Sundar Nagar, Old Suramangalam, Salem, Tamil Nadu. It's easily accessible and you can use the map above to get directions from your location.
